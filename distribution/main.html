Controls

This software is controlled primarily using mouse movement and the left mouse button:

View Controls

Pan View: Drag the background
Zoom View: Scroll using the mouse Wheel

Component Controls

Undo Action: Ctrl-z
Redo Action: Ctrl-y
Select Component: Click
Move Component: Click with Drag
Extend Component: Drag a joint (indicated by a blue circle)
Create Wire: Drag from a Hole
Add WireJoint: Double click a Wire
Remove WireJoint: Double click a WireJoint OR Drag a WireJoint over another
Remove Wire: Drag one of the last two WireJoints over the other and release
Recolour Wire: Select Wire and Click colour indicator
Rotate (Boards & OpAmp): Double click component
